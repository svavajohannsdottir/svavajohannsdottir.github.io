Svava Jóhannsdóttir | Noise Aesthetics


The next step. Where do I go from here. The options form an area, within a radius around me. What I end up doing is random, with an underlying hope; an unpredictability within isomorphism. A curve made in time, will I go up or down; as for overall I'm going down. A drop of water in a river, eddied along randomly but down the river; a piece of air in wind—and the air's moods spread to the objects it meets.

An ongoing study.
